Who We Are

Crafting Homes With Purpose

AP+AS Designs is a dedicated architectural and design firm committed to making quality home plans accessible to every Filipino family. We believe that every family deserves a beautiful, functional, and well-planned home — without breaking the bank.

We offer a complete set of signed and sealed architectural plans, from design all the way to building permit documentation. Our team is passionate about transforming your vision into detailed, permit-ready blueprints.
